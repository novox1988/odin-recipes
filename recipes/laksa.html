Laksa


Tasty Chicken Dish

A chicken dish that I eat every week

Ingredients

  • Two Noodle Nests
  • 300g Chicken Thigh
  • One Red Chilli
  • One Chicken Stock Cube
  • 200ml Coconut Milk
  • One Red Thai Chilli Paste Pot

Recipe

  1. Brown the Chicken
  2. Chop the chilli, and chuck it in
  3. Chuck the paste in, fry for 1 minute
  4. Add the coconut milk
  5. Add the stock cube
  6. Add the nests, wait till cooked and serve
